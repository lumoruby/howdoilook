The Journey to Finding Your Personal Style: 5 Essential Steps

Having your own style is a powerful tool for self-expression, beyond just dressing well. However, in a myriad of trends, finding 'your own style' can be challenging. If you're unsure where to begin, follow these 5 steps.

1. Collect Inspiration

The first step is to find styles that resonate with you. Use various media like Pinterest, Instagram, and fashion magazines to gather images you like. Anything is fine: specific people, color schemes, silhouettes, or moods. These collected images will serve as important clues to understanding your preferences. Creating a 'style board' or 'mood board' is also a great method.

2. Analyze Your Wardrobe

Now it's time to open your closet. Take out all your clothes and see which ones you wear frequently and which ones you never wear. There will surely be commonalities in the clothes you often reach for. You can identify your preferred elements such as comfortable materials, specific colors, or certain fits. Conversely, unworn clothes can tell you which styles you want to avoid.

3. Understand Yourself

Style reflects your lifestyle and values. Consider your profession, how you spend most of your time, and your hobbies. Someone with an active job might need comfortable and functional clothes, while someone in a creative field might try bolder, more unique styles. It's important to look inward through questions like 'Who am I?' and 'What do I value?'

4. Define Core Items

Considering your inspiration, wardrobe, and lifestyle comprehensively, define a few 'core items' that will represent your style. For example, 'well-fitting jeans,' 'high-quality white T-shirt,' or 'classic blazer.' These core items will anchor your wardrobe and serve as the foundation for various styling options.

5. Experiment and Evolve

Style is not something that's completed all at once. It is a continuous process of experimentation and evolution. Try new combinations and challenge yourself with styles you don't usually wear. Sometimes you might fail, but through that process, your style will become more solid and profound. Even starting by changing a small accessory can be beneficial. Having confidence and enjoying the process is most important.

Through these 5 steps, you will no longer blindly chase trends but discover the joy of creating your own style based on your standards. Find My Style supports your journey!
